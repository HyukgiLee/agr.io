21st Century Scholars (Purdue TFCS)

Purdue's 21st Century Scholars program helps students succeed in college and graduate through financial support and personalized coaching.

Key Features: Financial assistance, one-on-one coaching with professional staff, and access to various resources and counseling programs.

Purdue Promise

Purdue Promise provides financial aid and individual coaching to eligible Indiana students, ensuring they graduate on time without debt at Purdue.

Services Provided: One-on-one coaching, a safe counseling space, first-year transition support, and comprehensive assistance.
